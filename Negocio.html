THE BUSINESS OF BEE


Beekeepers in the United States found a great business in renting bees for pollination, so much so that they stopped producing honey, to import it from countries like China and, to a lesser extent, from Mexico. In Mexico, bees are also sold and rented for pollination work and for the improvement of hives.
